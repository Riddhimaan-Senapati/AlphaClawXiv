# Code of Conduct

AlphaClawXiv follows a simple contributor standard: be direct, respectful, and technical. Disagreement is fine; harassment, personal attacks, and bad-faith
participation are not.

## Expected Behavior

- Keep discussion focused on the project and the technical issue.
- Assume contributors are acting in good faith unless there is clear evidence otherwise.
- Provide actionable feedback in reviews and issues.
- Respect maintainers' decisions on scope, security, and release timing.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, threats, or discriminatory language.
- Posting private credentials, tokens, or personal data.
- Repeatedly derailing issues or pull requests.
- Demanding unpaid support outside the project scope.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may edit or delete comments, close issues, reject pull requests, or block users who violate these expectations.
